The Art Connection

Art is what makes our lives rich. We are surrounded by art all the time: whether it is a view of a serene lake or portrait of a folk dancer; in this ebb and flow of life, we rest our souls to take refuge in the world of arts. The diverse and vibrant culture of Gujarat spread across the state, from folk music to the delightful dance forms, from Rogan and Warli paintings to the glorious sculptures, the art in all its forms compliments our lives.

Art Exhibitions in our Gallery

With our first inaugural event from February 5th to the 15th, where we displayed our own paintings - it has been a great start. There were more than a 100 people visiting the gallery on a daily basis and most of the artwork that we had displayed was sold. We invite artists from around here to contact us for an exclusive or even an inclusive exhibition event where we can display their art and promote the event.

Marufhyder Jafri

Growing up in a culturally rich Sufi family, Maruf has always been surrounded by and inclined towards fine arts from his childhood. A post graduate in Business Administration with a specialization in Human Resources, and a stint in the Hotel Industry for about 10 years, Maruf returned to his passion of paintings. His subjects include nature landscapes and birds, espcially sparrows. He is also a corporate trainer and a visiting lecturer in the Maktabah Jafariyah Knowledge And Research Academy.

Ahmed Abbas Balwa

Ahmed has many awards and accolades to his credit. He was born in the village of "Sedrana" near Siddhpur, Kalangari, Anart region of Gujarat. He has had a passion for painting since childhood. After completing his primary education in Sedrana and secondary education in Shri LS High School Siddhpur, he joined The New Progressive Fine Arts College, Mehsana, inspired by the school's art teacher Mr HR Solanki, where he graduated with a Diploma in Fine Arts. He specializes in arts such as visual arts, portraits, motifs, word pictures, calligraphy or applied art, watercolors, oilcolors or pencils. He has mastered the art in every medium. He has done many camps through Lalit Kala and Kala Pratishthan, Surat, and has won Gold Position and Best Entry Award in International Art Online Competition, 2-National Award and 2-State Awarded Honors, as well as a World Record. His paintings have been collected in Gujarat, Mumbai, London, Daman, Goa and Rajasthan.
