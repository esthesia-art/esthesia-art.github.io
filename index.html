About Us

Located in the historical city of Sidhpur in North Gujarat, Esthesia Art Gallery was founded in 2021 with the coming together of two artists: Maruf Hyder Jafri and Ahmed Abbas Balwa. Maruf, a self taught artist is an alumni of M.S. University of Baroda; while Ahmed did his Diploma in Fine Arts from The New Progressive Fine Arts College, Mehsana.

The Gallery envisions bringing fine arts to the people of North Gujarat, and promoting artists in the region. It hosted its first ever event, an exhibition of art from the founders - from 5th February to 10th February where both Maruf and Ahmed displayed and sold their artwork.

In the future, Esthesia Art Gallery will be hosting and displaying many of the local talents of Gujarat. And will organise events for artists from other walks of life.

We encourage all artists, far and near, to reach out to us to promote their art in our region as we plan to organise art shows and events in our gallery and promote the artworks online. Participants will have their own dedicated webpage on our website with the pictures of their work and a short biography.
